Membership Tiers

Premium Members get a guaranteed seat at the board, directly influencing the direction of the growth through funds allocation. General Members elect one representative at the board as a class. Affiliate Members can join for free but have no voting rights. You can review the seL4 Foundation Directed Fund Participation Agreement beforehand (this is for review only, not for signing). If you have any questions, contact us at foundation@sel4.systems.

Note that seL4 Foundation members must be or become members of the Linux Foundation. The online process will include your Linux Foundation membership if you are not already a member.

The Linux Foundation tier can be the lowest available, e.g., seL4 Foundation Premium and General Members only need to be Linux Foundation Silver Members:

  • USD 20K/year (5000+)
  • USD 15K/year (500-4,999)
  • USD 10K/year (100-499)
  • USD 5k (1-99)
