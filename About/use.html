seL4 is used across a wide range of sectors

seL4's formally verified isolation enforcement enables a unique solution for safely running critical applications alongside any uncritical workloads. This capability has a significant impact across critical sectors such as automotive, aerospace, infrastructure, defense, secure communications, and more. Here are some examples of where seL4 is being used. There are many more: seL4 is open source and many products or development are not or cannot be public. If you would like to be added to this list, please contact us at foundation@sel4.systems. See also our list of research organisations conducting seL4-related research and our list of frameworks and languages built on or for seL4.
