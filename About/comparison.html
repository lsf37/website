Compared to other operating system solutions, seL4's uniqueness lies in its comprehensive formal correctness and security proofs while being the fastest operating system kernel available on IPC performance. It is a general-purpose and capability-based kernel, supporting hardware with Memory Management Unit (MMU). It is available as open source and is deployed in a wide range of sectors.
