seL4 Hangout

We host an open video chat hangout every two weeks to bring seL4 developers together. Dates/times are shown in seL4 calendar entry.

There is no strict agenda, anybody can join for technical discussions about seL4, related repositories and projects, or general questions and answers.

Members from the technical steering committee (TSC) will try to be there, but these are not TSC meetings where formal decisions are made.
