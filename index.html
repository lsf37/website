The world's most highly assured and fastest operating system kernel
Learn more about seL4 Start developing
{% include feature.html h2="Award-winning technology backed by ground-breaking research" text="

seL4 is both the world's most highly assured and the world's fastest operating system kernel. Its uniqueness lies in the formal mathematical proof that it behaves exactly as specified, enforcing strong security boundaries for applications running on top of it while maintaining the high performance that deployed systems need.

seL4 is grounded in research breakthroughs across multiple science disciplines. These breakthroughs have been recognised by international acclaimed awards, from the MIT Technology Review Award, to the ACM Hall of Fame Award, the ACM Software Systems Award, the DARPA Game changer award, and more.

" link="Research/" img="images/tries-shutterstock/award.jpeg" alt="ACM SIGOPS award" %} {% include feature.html right=true h2="Protecting critical systems around the globe" text="

seL4 protects critical systems from software failures and cyber-attacks. It allows non-critical functionality to run securely alongside critical payloads by enforcing strong isolation and controlled communication.

seL4 is used in a wide range of critical sectors, from automotive, aerospace and IoT to data distribution, military and intelligence. It has been successfully retrofitted into complex critical systems and has demonstrably prevented cyber-attacks. Government organisations on several continents have funded further development of seL4 and its ecosystem.

" link="#" img="images/tries-shutterstock/cpu.jpg" alt="Critical system" %} {% include feature.html h2="Supported by commercial service providers" text="

seL4 is the leading choice for building highly reliable software. Commercial support is available to help you build or migrate your product to run on seL4 and benefit from its unparalleled security.

A number of Trusted Service Providers have been endorsed by the seL4 Foundation for their expertise and experience in systems and/or formal verification at various levels: kernel, kernel platform ports, user-level Operating Systems components, and applications.

" link="#" img="images/tries-shutterstock/providers.jpg" alt="Commercial support" %} {% include feature.html right=true h2="Backed by an Open Source Foundation" text="

seL4 is open source, supported by the seL4 Foundation, an open, transparent and neutral organisation, whose aim is to ensure that seL4 is not only the world's best secure operating-system technology, but is also readily deployable and supported by a diverse and stable ecosystem of services and products.

seL4 is free to use. Foundation memberships are supporting the maintenance and further development of the seL4 microkernel and related technologies, as well as the community of seL4 users, developers, contributors, providers and adopters.

" link="Foundation/" img="images/tries-shutterstock/foundation.png" alt="seL4 Foundation" %} {% include feature.html h2="Contributions from a strong ecosystem" text="

seL4 and the related technologies that are maintained by the seL4 Foundation are distributed across dozens of repositories, with contributions from developers around the world.

While the microkernel code itself evolves through a tightly controlled process, safeguarded by the Foundation's Technical bodies, to preserve its security, high assurance, and mathematical proofs, contributions to supporting frameworks, tools, and components are moving faster, increasing the ease of adoption and migration.

" link="#" img="images/tries-shutterstock/world.jpg" alt="Ecosystem" %} {% include feature.html right=true h2="Annual gathering at the seL4 summit" text="

The seL4 Summit is the annual international conference on the seL4 microkernel and all seL4-related technology, tools, infrastructure, products, projects, and people.

It aims to bring together the entire seL4 community to learn about the seL4 technology, its latest advances, uses, successes, challenges and plans. The event showcases exciting seL4 development, research, real world applications and experiences, offering an opportunity to connect with other seL4 developers, users, providers, customers, supporters, potential partners and enthusiasts.

" link="#" img="images/tries-shutterstock/summit.jpg" alt="seL4 summit" %} {% include feature.html h2="Development Roadmap" text="

With an active, public development roadmap, seL4 continues to solidify its position as the leading secure operating system and the industry standard for verified software.

As seL4 gains wider adoption, it continues to evolve, expanding its support for an ever-growing range of platforms, architectures, configurations and features.

In addition, a growing ecosystem of frameworks, tools, components and language support is being developed to facilitate the production of seL4-based systems.

Finally, since verification is what makes seL4 unique, as the seL4 code evolves, so does its formal proofs, which are constantly maintained and kept in lock-step with the code.

Check out our comprehensive dedicated Docsite - it provides detailed technical documentation, tutorials to get started, cheat sheets, how-to guides, and more.
