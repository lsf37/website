A growing community of experts offer consulting and development services to support seL4 projects. The seL4 Foundation provides guidance by endorsing service providers for their seL4-related expertise, either in systems or in verification. If you are a member interested in being endorsed, email foundation@sel4.systems.

' layout: card redirect_from: /Foundation/Services/endorsement.html --- {% include section-start.html id = "endorsement" heading = "Services and endorsements" intro = '

The type of services that the member has expertise on and is being endorsed for, are described in detail, and taken from a template list of services:

  • systems: kernel
  • systems: kernel platform ports
  • systems: user-level OS
  • systems: applications
  • verification: kernel
  • verification: user-level OS
  • verification: applications

Endorsement in one of the categories above means that the member has one or multiple people in-house who have that expertise and have already contributed in this area. This is reviewed annually.

Each category can have qualifiers/examples, which can be suggested by the member, eg "user-level OS (VM)” or “user-level OS (CAmkES).”

The endorsement of services is limited to members and provided for free.
